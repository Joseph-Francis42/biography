Chronology

Career Milestones

1964

The Jackson 5

Michael begins performing with his brothers, quickly capturing the world's attention with his raw talent, extraordinary vocal range, and charismatic stage presence.

1979

Going Solo: Off the Wall

Launches his breakthrough adult solo album, producing four Top 10 hits and showcasing his unique blend of disco, pop, and R&B, establishing him as a solo force.

1982

The Thriller Phenomenon

Releases 'Thriller', redefining the global music industry, breaking racial barriers on television, and debuting the iconic Moonwalk during the Motown 25 broadcast.

1991

Dangerous & Beyond

Continues his global reign with albums like 'Dangerous' and 'HIStory', blending socially conscious messages with cutting-edge pop production and massive world tours.

Legacy

The King of Pop's Legacy

Inducted twice into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame. Michael Jackson remains one of the most influential dancers, vocalists, and cultural figures of the 20th century.
